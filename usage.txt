Usage:

var parser = new NameParse();
var parsed = parser.parse("Mr. Chales P. Wooten, III");

Results:

parsed {
   salutation: "Mr.",
   firstName: "Charles",
   initials: "P",
   lastName: "Wooten",
   suffix: "III"
}
